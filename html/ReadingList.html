List of Recommended Books

Physics

  • Understanding Physics, by Isaac Asimov
  • The Theoretical Minimum, by Leonard Susskind
  • The Road to Reality, by Roger Penrose
  • Introducing Quantum Theory, an awesome comic introduction, which rewards those readers who already know the physics
  • Physics for Scientists and Engineers, by Knight
  • Six Easy Pieces, by Richard Feynman
  • Six Not So Easy Pieces, by Richard Feynman
  • Facts and Mysteries in Elementary Particle Physics, by M. Veltman

Mathematics

  • Calculus, by Taalman and Kohn
  • Elements, by Euclid
  • All the works of Martin Gardner
  • Algebra, by Artin
  • ForAllx, by Magnus

Fiction

  • The Three Body Problem/Rememberance of Earth's Past Trilogy, by Cixin Liu
  • Infinite Jest, David Foster Wallace
  • Dune, by Frank Herbert
  • Crime and Punishment, by Dostoevsky
  • The Death of Ivan Ilyich, by Tolstoy
  • What If?, by Randall Munroe
  • Farenheit 451

Non-Fiction, Political Science, Philosophy, and Economics

  • Kill All Normies, by Angela Nagle
  • The Rebel Sell, by Heath and Potter
  • Green Illusions, by Ozzie Zehner
  • Justice, by Michael Sandel
  • Ain't Nobody's Business if you Do, by Peter Mcwilliams
  • Strategy, by Freedman
  • The Utility of Force, by Rupert Smith
  • Collapse, by Jared Diamond
  • On Liberty, by Mills
  • Anarchy, the State, and Utopia, by Nozick
  • On Tyranny, by Snyder
  • On War, by Clausewitz
  • The Trial and Death of Socrates, by Plato
